# OpenPlanTrace Roadmap

OpenPlanTrace is a standalone plan intelligence engine. It must never depend on or modify any downstream application. Downstream apps consume exported results or library APIs.

## Principles

- Do not fake AI or semantic detections.
- Prefer measurable geometry evidence before classification.
- Keep loaders optional and format-specific.
- Keep the core scanner normalized around `PlanDocument`.
- Every major detector needs diagnostics, confidence, and visual review support.
- Every production feature needs fixtures or evaluation data.
